Please read our disclosure policy.\n\nA classic turkey sandwich recipe is perfect for game day or holiday leftovers!\n\nPiled high with turkey, lettuce, tomato, cheese, and a liberal amount of cranberry sauce, this turkey sandwich recipe is delicious!\n\nTurkey Sandwich (with Cranberry Sauce!)\nTurkey and cranberry sauce are a perfect pair, and these turkey cranberry sandwiches are next-level easy to make!\nEnjoy them cold, or serve them hot panini-style!\nGo for gourmet! Make your turkey sandwich ‘bistro-style’ and try variations of mayo aiolis like homemade garlic aioli and unique toppings like crispy fried onions or pickled red onions.\nWhat to Put on a Turkey Sandwich\n\nBread: Choose a sturdy bread like a nice grain bread, ciabatta, or even rye or sourdough. Want something lighter? Wrap the ingredients in romaine lettuce!\n\nTurkey: Use sliced deli turkey or leftover sliced turkey breast. Add other deli meats like ham, chicken, or cotto salami for variety.\n\nMY LATEST VIDEOS\nInstant Pot Beef Stew\nInstant Pot Beef Stew\n0 seconds of 1 minute, 52 secondsVolume 0%\n \n\nCheese: Choose your cheese, great options include cheddar, Colby Jack, Monterey Jack, or Gouda. Mix and match for flavor.\n\nVegetables: Add crunch with lettuce, tomatoes, or cucumbers. For creaminess, try sliced avocado.\n\nCondiments: Mix mayo with pesto for aioli, or use chipotle for a kick. Honey mustard adds a sweet tang.\n\nMore Turkey Sandwich Ideas\nHot Open Face – Top slices of turkey on bread, crumble on some dressing, and smother in gravy, like this hot turkey sandwich.\nPlain Grilled – Turkey, deli ham slices, American cheese, and tomato on rye bread, pumpernickel, or your favorite bread. Cook it grilled cheese style until melty!\nTurkey Reuben Panini – If you have a panini press, pile on the turkey, Swiss cheese, and sauerkraut on rye bread slices that have been buttered on the outside, and cook to a golden crispy crunch. Grilling in a skillet will work too. Serve this one with Thousand Island dressing on the side.\nTurkey Salad – Make an easy turkey salad recipe and fill croissants or toasted English muffins!\nLeftover Turkey Faves\nEasy Turkey Soup\n\nSoups & Stews\n\nTurkey Pot Pie\n\nTurkey\n\nTurkey Tetrazzini\n\nRecipes\n\nWhite Turkey Chili\n\nSlow Cooker\n\nDid you make this Turkey Sandwich?
